# Data Warehouse Design Exercise

## Scenario

You are designing a data warehouse for an e-commerce company.

Source systems contain:

- Customers
- Orders
- Products
- Payments

The business wants analytics for:

- Revenue
- Customer behavior
- Product performance

## Questions

1. What fact table(s) would you create?

2. What dimension table(s) would you create?

3. What would be the grain of your fact table?

4. How would you handle customer address changes?

5. Draw a simple star schema.

6. How would you scale this solution if the company grew from 1 million to 100 million orders?

7. New orders arrive every 15 minutes.

8. Describe how you would monitor data quality in your warehouse.

Examples:
- Duplicate records
- Missing data
- Unexpected values
- Failed loads

How would you load this data into your warehouse without reprocessing all historical records?
